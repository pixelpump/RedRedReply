How it works

  1. Navigate to any Reddit post (e.g. reddit.com/r/something/comments/…)
  2. Click the Reddit Reply AI extension icon in your toolbar
  3. Choose a tone for your reply
  4. Optionally add context or instructions
  5. Click Generate and copy your reply
